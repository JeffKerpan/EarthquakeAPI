About Jump Note

Jump Note is an application that allows you to take time stamped notes on a video of your choice.

Jeff Kerpan

Before becoming a Full Stack Web Developer, Jeff obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Finance from the University of Missouri-Columbia. Following graduation he worked in Banking, Investments, Insurance and Mortgages for 10 years building an analytical mindset. He has always had a passing for computers and programming in general. In his spare time he enjoys mountain and road biking, hiking and skiing.
